About Our ToDo List Project

Introduction

Welcome to our ToDo List Project! This application is designed to help you manage your tasks efficiently, ensuring you stay organized and productive. Whether you're juggling multiple projects at work, keeping track of personal errands, or planning your study schedule, our ToDo List app is here to streamline your task management process.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower individuals and teams to achieve their goals through effective task management. We believe that a well-organized plan is key to success, and our ToDo List app is designed to provide the tools needed to stay on top of your responsibilities and deadlines.

Technology Stack

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React.js for a dynamic and responsive user interface.
  • Backend: Node.js and Express.js for a robust and scalable server-side application.
  • Database: MongoDB for efficient and flexible data storage.
  • Hosting: Deployed on a reliable cloud platform for high availability and performance.
